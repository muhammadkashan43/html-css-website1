Mount Kilimanjaro

Mount Kilimanjaro, located in Tanzania, is Africa's highest peak at 5,895 meters (19,341 feet). It's a free-standing volcanic mountain with three cones: Kibo, Mawenzi, and Shira.

Mount Popocatépetl

Mount Popocatépetl is an active stratovolcano located in central Mexico. It is the second-highest peak in Mexico, standing at 5,426 meters (17,802 feet) above sea level.
